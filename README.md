# 🪔 Diwali Sales Analysis

## 📌 Objective
Analyze customer purchasing behavior during Diwali to identify high-value customers and top-selling product categories.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Customer demographics
- Product categories
- Purchase amount
- Location data

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Women aged 26-35 are the highest purchasing group
- Top selling categories:
  - Clothing
  - Electronics
  - Food
- Highest sales from:
  - Uttar Pradesh
  - Maharashtra
  - Karnataka

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Target women aged 26-35 for marketing campaigns
- Focus on high-demand product categories
- Improve marketing in top-performing states

---

## ✅ Conclusion
This project helps in understanding customer behavior and improving sales strategies during festive seasons.
